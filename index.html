--- layout: home search_exclude: true image: images/logo.png --- ## Objectives

This Course Framework teaches Java, College Boards 10 Units of AP Computer Science A, and Web Site application development in an integrated fashion. The purpose is to integrate College Board requirements with Career Technical Education (CTE) needs. The outcome is High School students prepared for the AP Test and be ready for Computer Science intenships.
